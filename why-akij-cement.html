Why Akij Cement

Akij Cement has gained the trust and confidence of the Individual Home Builders for its uncompromising quality. ACCL
produces both OPC, PCC & PCC for its customers.

100-years-assurance

Akij Cement's "100-years-assurance" represents their commitment to providing cement that is built to last. It signifies the confidence they have in the quality and strength of their cement, ensuring that structures constructed using Akij Cement will stand the test of time.

Go green

Akij Cement, a prominent cement brand, actively promotes the "go green" ethos in its operations and products. The company emphasizes sustainability and environmental responsibility throughout its manufacturing processes. Akij Cement prioritizes the efficient use of natural resources, reduction of carbon emissions, and proper waste management.

Test reports

Akij Cement's test reports provide detailed information on the physical and chemical properties of their cement products. These reports cover essential parameters such as compressive strength, setting time, fineness, soundness, and chemical composition. The testing procedures follow established protocols.

Certificates

Akij Cement, a leading cement brand, obtains various certificates to demonstrate its commitment to quality, environmental sustainability, and compliance with industry standards. Here are some certificates: ISO 9001, ISO 14001, BSTI Certification, OHSAS 18001, BUET certification.
